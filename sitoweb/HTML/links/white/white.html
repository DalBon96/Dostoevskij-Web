The White Nights

Published in 1848

Is a romantic short story about a lonely young man, referred to as the dreamer,
who wanders the streets of St. Petersburg during the city's "white nights".
One evening, he meets Nastenka, a young woman in distress, and they form a brief but intense connection.
Over the course of four nights, the dreamer falls in love with Nastenka,
but she is waiting for the return of another man whom she loves. Despite his feelings,
the dreamer helps her reunite with her lover, leading to a bittersweet ending as he returns to his solitary life.
