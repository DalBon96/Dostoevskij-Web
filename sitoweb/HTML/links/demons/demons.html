The Demons

Published in 1872

Set in a provincial Russian town, it explores the rise of radical ideologies
and the destructive effects of nihilism on individuals and society.
The novel revolves around a group of revolutionaries who plot to incite chaos,
with characters symbolizing different facets of moral and political thought.
Through its depiction of moral decay and ideological extremism,
The Demons serves as a critique of the intellectual movements in 19th-century Russia.
