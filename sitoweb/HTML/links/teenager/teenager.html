The Teenager

Published in 1875

It follows Arkady Dolgoruky, a 19-year-old illegitimate son of a landowner,
who dreams of asserting his independence and becoming wealthy through a "grand idea."
The novel is narrated by Arkady as he struggles with his identity,
torn between his desire for power and the influence of his father, Versilov, a man he both admires and despises.
